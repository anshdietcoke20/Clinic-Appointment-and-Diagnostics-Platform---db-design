<h1>Clinic Appointment and Diagnostics Platform</h1>
I have created an  ER diagram for a clinic system

<h3>Highlights</h3>
<p> - sections for patients, doctors, appointments, consultations, diagnostic tests, payments, etc. </p>
<p> - patients can visit multiple doctors </p>
<p> - doctors have specialities </p>
<p> - appointments can be booked </p>
<p> - consulations can lead to diagnostic tests or not </p>
<p> - tests can be prescribed, one patient can have multiple tests </p>
<p> - tests reports can be generated, one test can lead to many reports </p>
<p> - single payment will lead to single appointment </p>
